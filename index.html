Wanna Stay Wild ! 900 Kandi...Let's get it!! With dazzling picturesque and greenary the scenic beauty of 900 Kandi is at the next level. The flaura and fauna of this panoramic forest is just gonna make you awe !! A place with abundant scenery and striking beauty, 900 Kandi is a forest in Wayanad. Are you adventurous? Do you enjoy the striking visuals of forest? Would you like to trek in a forest which are free reins of elephant? Do you enjoy the sound of brook whick breaks the hush forest? Would you love to spend your night in an enchanted mud house? Would you like to kick start your mist dawn with the melodious voice of cuckoo? A place which conjoints answers to all of the above , a place just to make you feel content and wholesome with its charming picturesque, WELCOME , 900 Kandi awaits you just to give you exceptional memories In Wayand one the best place to explore is 900 KANDI

900 Kandi (Thollayiram Kandi) is a tourist destination located in Meppadi village of Wayanad district, Kerala.[1] Thollayiram in local parlance means “900” and Kandi means “a patch of land". It is one of the hidden areas in the forest of Wayanad and are not visited by many tourists.[2] The off-road trekking through the middle of the forest and a glass bridge located here are the major attraction at 900 Kandi.[3] Overview A view of 900 Kandi from a resort 900 Kandi was mostly unknown to common. people until the release of Lord Livingstone 7000 Kandi in 2015, which was shot here. In the local language 'Kandi' means a field.[4] 900 Kandi is the name of the estate situated within the forest land. It lies within the dense forests of Wayanad.[5] The 900 acres of land here was a farm belonging to one of the settlers, Parel Pappan, decades ago. After that, this area was called as 900 Kandi.[6] Despite the area's farmland has expanded beyond 900 acres, the name still lives on. Spread over acres of estates and forest lands, the journey to 900 Kandi is through the forests.[7] Location The journey to 900 Kandi begins beyond the first bridge after Kalladi Maqam on the way from Meppadi to Soochipara Falls. 900 Kandi is located at a distance of 14 km from Meppadi.[8] Bus facility is available to Meppadi which is 12 km from Kalpatta town. Private jeep services are available from Meppadi to 900 Kandi. The nearest railway station from Meppadi is Kozhikode station which is 78 km away and the nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, which is 87 km away.[9]
